Humacao is a municipality in Puerto Rico located in the eastern coast of the island, north of Yabucoa; south of Naguabo; 
east of Las Piedras; and west of Vieques Passage. Humacao is spread over 12 barrios and Humacao barrio-pueblo. It is part 
of the San Juan-Caguas-Guaynabo Metropolitan Statistical Area. Wikipedia

Population: 52,507 (2019)
